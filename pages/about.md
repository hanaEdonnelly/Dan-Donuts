<br>
Dan and the Donuts are Charleston’s premier 50s rock ’n’ roll party band, dedicated to keeping the golden era of dancehall music alive and thriving. Inspired by the golden days of rock music, the band delivers a sound that instantly transports audiences back to jukeboxes, soda shops, and packed wooden dance floors.

From weddings to bars to private parties, Dan and the Donuts value being professional and easy to work with. Polished and passionate about their craft, Dan and the Donuts bring timeless style, nonstop energy, and a whole lot of fun to every stage they step on---proving that great music never goes out of style.
